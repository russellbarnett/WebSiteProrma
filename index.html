What BrandStudios.AI
Does

BrandStudios.AI is an orchestration and governance layer that sits above your existing creative tools.

It learns what brand-grade looks like by analyzing your best approved work, then evaluates AI-generated assets against that standard before they ship.

Orchestrate creation across your stack.
Score AI outputs against your best work.
Route edge cases for human approval.

On-brand is table stakes. The real risk is “technically compliant” work that still feels wrong at scale. This system is built to catch that gap.

How BrandStudios.AI Works

Five steps from brand capture to governed release—with humans in the loop where it matters.

1

Build your Brand DNA

Your best approved work becomes the reference. Not guidelines. Examples of what “brand-grade” actually looks like.

Ads and campaigns Emails Social content Design systems Copy and messaging
2

Create content using your existing tools

Your teams keep working as they do today. BrandStudios.AI fits above your stack without replacing it.

ChatGPT Claude Gemini Adobe Figma Canva Agencies
3

Automatic evaluation of AI-generated assets

AI-generated content is evaluated against your Brand DNA using two measures. These scores make quality measurable and drift visible—before anything ships.

Brand Grade
0 to 100. How closely the asset matches your best approved work.
Brand Drift
Low to high. Where tone, structure, layout, or message begins to move.
4

Smart approvals and routing

Based on Brand Grade and Brand Drift, work follows the right approval path: auto-approve clear matches, send edge cases for human approval, and flag what needs revision.

Auto-Approved Human Approval Flagged

Humans stay in control. The system removes the busywork.

5

Continuous learning

Every approval, edit, and rejection becomes a learning signal. Over time, scoring tightens and the review burden drops—without lowering the bar.
